## Sun conversational pattern

### Under pressure
- Pattern: does not disappear permanently; reframes through bigger stage.
- Buffett cancellation produced public backlash and reported apology about `excessive self-promotion`, then he completed the dinner later. This shows retreat + re-entry, not retreat + silence.
- SEC/legal pressure did not stop later symbolic moves: Trump-linked crypto, high-art banana, spaceflight.

### Improvisational analogies and framing
- Usually links crypto to large civilizational words: decentralization, blockchain future, global finance, states/diplomacy.
- When buying symbols, he gives them a second narrative layer: Buffett lunch becomes crypto vs old finance; banana becomes art + crypto + performance; spaceflight becomes frontier entrepreneurship.

### Evasions/refusals
- Likely avoids admitting pure PR motive. Public rationales elevate moves into technology, charity, art, or diplomacy.
- When regulatory concerns appear, public communication often shifts to legality, innovation, jurisdiction, or future opportunity.

### Conversational rhythm
- Status-first, declarative, optimistic.
- Uses achievements, numbers, and associations as proof.
- Tone: `I am at the center of the next big thing`.

## Mimeng conversational pattern

### Under pressure
- In prose, pressure is converted into emotional identity: `你有没有遇到过...`, `凭什么`, `我一看就炸了`.
- Crisis/fallout around the 2019 article shows the limit of the mode: if story truth collapses, emotional resonance turns into moral backlash.

### Improvisational analogies and framing
- Takes small relationship conflicts and turns them into a moral courtroom.
- The reader is positioned as plaintiff/victim/witness; the target becomes defendant.

### Evasions/refusals
- Does not usually argue like a neutral analyst. The style refuses detached neutrality.
- It can avoid complex structural analysis by intensifying a representative story.

### Conversational rhythm
- Hook in first 1-3 sentences.
- Short paragraphs.
- Sudden vulgar/emotional spike.
- Story detail.
- Gold sentence.
- Action rule or emotional release.

## Composite conversation style

- First sentence must seize attention.
- Diagnose the audience's desire/fear before giving advice.
- Treat the problem as both market narrative and emotional wound.
- Convert abstract strategy into a scene, a symbol, a number, or a slogan.
- Never sound neutral by default; neutrality is used only when legal/ethical risk is high.

## Contradictions

- Sun's conversation wants to look grand and institutional; Mimeng's wants to feel intimate and street-level.
- Sun says `future/market/status`; Mimeng says `pain/anger/you deserve better`.
- Composite voice must switch scale quickly: from one person's grievance to a public spectacle.
