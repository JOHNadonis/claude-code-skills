## External views of Sun

### Strengths outsiders repeatedly notice
- Master of attention arbitrage.
- Excellent at attaching himself to powerful symbols and institutions.
- High tolerance for controversy.
- Fast cross-border capital/regulatory maneuvering.
- Builds or buys distribution: TRON, BitTorrent, exchanges, diplomacy, elite-network events.

### Criticisms
- Excessive self-promotion.
- Legal/regulatory allegations: unregistered securities, wash trading, celebrity promotion disclosures.
- Ethical concerns around market manipulation and customer funds, based on media allegations.
- Substance vs hype gap: critics see more narrative engineering than technical originality.
- Jurisdiction/status shopping: citizenship, residency, diplomacy, offshore exchanges.

### Defenses / alternative readings
- In crypto, narrative and network effects are real product forces.
- His moves often do create attention, liquidity, and community coordination.
- He survives cycles that kill quieter founders, which itself suggests strategic resilience.

## External views of Mimeng

### Strengths outsiders repeatedly notice
- Extremely sharp mass psychology sensor.
- Knows titles, hooks, scenes, and emotional pacing.
- Team-based industrialization of viral writing.
- Can convert ordinary frustrations into shareable public language.

### Criticisms
- Anxiety-selling.
- Emotional manipulation.
- Gender/relationship conflict amplification.
- Story authenticity problems, especially after the 2019 `寒门状元` fallout.
- Over-optimization for 10w+ can damage truth, nuance, and public trust.

### Defenses / alternative readings
- She identified genuine pain points ignored by polite mainstream writing.
- Her methods are effective because they locate existing emotions rather than inventing them from nothing.
- In content markets, emotional accuracy often beats formal correctness.

## Composite external reputation

If combined, outsiders would likely call it:
- A traffic war machine.
- A masterclass in attention leverage.
- Dangerous if not constrained by truth discipline.
- Brilliant at packaging desire, grievance, and status into campaigns.

## Core criticism to preserve in the skill

This perspective must not be sanitized. Its power comes with moral risk:
- It can exploit pain.
- It can turn people into props.
- It can confuse liquidity/virality with value.
- It can mistake survival after backlash for being right.

## Contradictions

- Both are criticized for manipulation, yet both succeed because they understand real human incentives.
- Their critics focus on ethics; their fans/followers often focus on results.
- The skill should expose both: effectiveness and danger.
