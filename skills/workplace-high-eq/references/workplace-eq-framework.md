# Workplace High EQ Framework

## Thesis

Workplace high EQ is not universal niceness. It is goal-directed relationship management built on value, boundary, power, empathy, and communication.

## Anti-Misread

- Low EQ is not always harshness; it can be over-agreeableness.
- High EQ is not treating everyone well; it is choosing who deserves energy.
- Relationship is not value. Good relationship without value can become unpaid workload.
- Value creates relationship leverage. Relationship without boundary creates capture.

## Relationship And Value

### Key Distinctions

- Having relationship does not prove value.
- Having value tends to create relationship need.
- Relationship should be adjusted through value, not guilt.
- User does not need everyone to like them.

### Value Sources At Work

1. Formal authority: title, approval rights, assignment rights.
2. Reward/punishment: performance review, bonus, promotion, blame.
3. Expertise: knowledge that solves hard problems.
4. Information: early or exclusive access to useful facts.
5. Charisma/reputation: trust, influence, credibility.
6. Scarcity/alternatives: ability to refuse because options exist.

## Boundary

Boundary means deciding what relationship does not include.

Common boundaries:

- Time: no unpaid rescue as default.
- Credit: no invisible labor.
- Responsibility: no taking blame for others' choices.
- Emotional load: no becoming complaint trash can.
- Authority: no accepting work from people without mandate.

## Saying No

Saying no has two jobs:

1. protects boundary
2. signals value and power

### No Formula

```text
认可对方处境 + 标明边界 + 给可承受选项 + 关门句
```

Example:

```text
我理解你这边今天确实急。但我手上这版周五要交，不能接完整任务。你可以把最卡的一个点发我，我下午给你 10 分钟建议，后续你自己推进。
```

## Seven-Part EQ Loop

### 1. Self-Awareness

Ask:

- 我在干嘛？
- 我在想啥？
- 我现在什么情绪？

Purpose: stop being dragged by emotion or guilt.

### 2. Self-Management

Ask:

- 我要做啥？
- 我想得到啥？

Purpose: move from venting to goal and action.

### 3. Other-Awareness

Ask:

- 他在干嘛？
- 他在想啥？
- 他现在什么情绪？
- 他真正需要什么？

Purpose: see motive, pressure, and leverage.

### 4. Other-Management

Ask:

- 我想让他干啥？
- 他为什么会愿意这么做？
- 我能给什么台阶或交换？

Purpose: design behavior change, not just express emotion.

### 5. Empathy

Empathy has two actions:

1. Say their emotion.
2. Ask their need.

Template:

```text
我听下来你现在主要是[情绪]，因为[处境]。你最需要我帮你解决的是哪一块？
```

### 6. Communication

Communication has two actions:

1. Say user's emotion.
2. Say user's need.

Template:

```text
我这边会担心[风险/情绪]，所以我需要[边界/动作]。我们可以按[方案]走。
```

### 7. Self-Reinforcement

After result, ask:

- 哪句话起作用？
- 哪个边界守住了？
- 下次遇到类似人，第一句该怎么说？

## Default Decision Labels

- Accept: high value, low boundary cost.
- Reject: low value, high boundary cost.
- Delay: value unclear, timing bad.
- Redirect: should be handled by another owner.
- Negotiate: relationship valuable but request too costly.
- Escalate: authority conflict, repeated abuse, policy issue.
- Ignore: low value, low risk noise.

## Response Style

- Be practical, not moralistic.
- Give wording, not abstract virtue.
- Preserve dignity without surrendering boundary.
- Avoid fake warmth. Respectful firmness beats soft compliance.
